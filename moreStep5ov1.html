Individuals must have a social security card to prove their identity and citizenship so that they may be eligible for many types of housing, including Section 8 and supportive housing. A person is required to at least have a copy of their social security card when applying for housing, and the original may be requested depending on program and housing type. This process can be difficult for a number of reasons.
